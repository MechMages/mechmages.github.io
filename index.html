The Mech Mages

Our Mission

The Mech Mages #24385, a second-year robotics team, consists of a diverse group of primarily high school upperclassmen. Under the Saint Louis Students Robotics Association, our mission is to recruit and mentor community youth, equipping them with valuable STEM skills such as computer programming, 3D design, and manufacturing. Additionally, we emphasize the development of soft skills like communication and business acumen. As active participants in the FIRST Tech Challenge, we proudly uphold FIRST's core values, including gracious professionalism and community service. Our team is dedicated to contributing to our community through various outreach projects, fostering a spirit of collaboration and innovation.

Our Team

Our team is composed of primarily high school students attending Lafayette Highschool in Wildwood Missouri. If you are interested in joining our team or getting in contact, please reach out to mechmages@gmail.com. We are always looking for younger players to keep the team going.
