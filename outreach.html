The Mech Mages team members volunteered at STEM night at Rockwood South Middle school on the 25th of April. Our team enjoyed interacting with the younger students and teaching them about engineering and design

Superbowl of Destruction at the Arch Reactor - 2024

The Mech Mages attend an annual electronics recycling event at a local “Hackerspace” where we recycled nearly $300 dollars worth of equipment! We were excited to interact with the memebrs of the Arch Reactor who were incredibly generous and kind to our team
