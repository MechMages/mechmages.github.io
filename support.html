Tax-Deductible Financial Support

To sponsor / donate to the Mech Mages, please do so through our fiscal sponsor the Saint Louis Students Robotics Association. There you will find a donate page. SLSRA is a nonprofit, 501(c)(3) organization, EIN# 81-2974421. Your donation is tax-deductible under IRC Section 170.

Once you've donated, make sure to contact us at MechMages@gmail.com with your donation amount, so we can feature your name or logo on our team material. This will also help with our accounting
